Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a refund?

Most refunds are processed within 24 hours of cancellation. However, depending on your bank or payment method, it may take 5-10 business days for the amount to reflect in your account.

Is there a cancellation fee?

Cancellation fees depend on the service provider's policy and how close to the booking date you cancel. You'll always see the applicable fees before confirming cancellation.

What if I need to cancel outside business hours?

Our cancellation system is available 24/7. You can cancel your booking at any time through our website or mobile app.

How can I check my refund status?

You can track your refund status in the "My Refunds" section of your account. Alternatively, you can call our toll-free number for assistance.
